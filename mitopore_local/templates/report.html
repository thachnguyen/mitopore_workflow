Variance calling summary

POS: Position in the mitochondrial genome; REF: Nucleotide in the reference sequence; ALT: Alternating nucleotide detected in the sample; GT: Genotype. 0 = Wildtype; 1, 2, 3 … = Different variants detected starting with the highest to the lowest frequency. AF: Allele Frequency. The level of each genotype other than wildtype in fractions of 0–1; DP: Depth: read depth at the indicated position.
